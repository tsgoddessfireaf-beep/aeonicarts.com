About

The tradition, kept intact.

Aeonic Arts is the practice of Dolores Puckett — traditional horary astrology done the way it was actually meant to be done. Horary is the branch that answers a single, sincere question by casting a chart for the moment the question is understood. It's precise, it's accountable, and it gives you a real answer instead of a flattering paragraph that could apply to anyone.

What "traditional" actually means

Most of what passes for astrology today is a 20th-century product: sun signs, twelve-word horoscopes, the Mercury-retrograde meme cycle. That's astrology stripped of its operating system. The tradition Aeonic Arts works from — William Lilly, Guido Bonatti, Dorotheus of Sidon — is the intact system: significators, essential dignity, aspects, reception, and the motion of the Moon, each part earning its place in the judgment.

How a reading is read

Every reading starts with your real question. The chart for that moment is the answer. The significators are identified, their condition assessed, and the way they come together — by aspect, by sign, by dignity — tells the story of how the matter resolves. You get a committed judgment, and an explanation of why.

Who it's for

This work is for grown women — including the over-40 and LGBTQ+ community — who want to be taken seriously. Intelligent, warm, inclusive, and never condescending. You bring a real question. You get a real answer.
